Who We Are ?

CA Rahul Godbole is an Associate Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. He practices in the small and medium scale enterprises segment. He is associated as a financial consultant to various business organizations in India

Rahul Godbole with his team of eminent professionals & expert staff endeavors to provide tailor made financial solutions to the clients. Along with the traditional practice of audit and taxation, we are specialized in developing MIS system & Project Management for various business organizations.

We are working with a wide range of clients from different industries and handled a variety of assignments for them. Our team is committed to provide the best of financial services to our clients.

CA Rahul Godbole has also been auditor of various government organizations. He has worked in association with various firms of chartered accountants in India. He also provides various advisory services to many NGOs in India.

Founder
